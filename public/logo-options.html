TruckX Logo Options

These are website-inspired directions for the dialer brand. Option C is installed in the web app, and Option B is installed for the Chrome extension.

A. Connected Wordmark

Closest to a clean TruckX product identity: dark wordmark, blue connected X, and fleet-tech signal lines.

B. Extension Mark

Best for Chrome extension icons, favicon, and small square spaces where the full wordmark is too wide.

C. Route Signal

More trucking/logistics focused. Good if the dialer should feel closer to fleet operations than pure sales software.

D. Compact Lockup

Good for an admin portal header when you want the icon and wordmark together in a tighter space.
